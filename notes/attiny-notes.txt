AVR ATtiny Microcontrollers:

ATtiny85 is the most powerful 8-bin chip:
http://www.atmel.com/dyn/products/product_card.asp?part_id=3612&category_id=163&family_id=607&subfamily_id=791


ATtiny13 is very similar, just less Flash:
http://www.atmel.com/dyn/products/product_card.asp?part_id=3175&category_id=163&family_id=607&subfamily_id=791
